Why Eidetic?

Pathways over specific information

By now it's no secret that the way we process and store information is changing. The digital era has warranted prioritizing remembering how to find information over the specific information itself. We believe that if your application attends to all available metadata that it can when you create notes, it should use that information to help you find them when you them.

Let's Talk Privacy

Collecting as much metadata as possible allows Eidetic to understand the way your brain works in ways that might not be immediately apparent to you. No identifying information of your is ever saved on remote servers. The more data the app has access to, the more magical its suggestions will seem.
