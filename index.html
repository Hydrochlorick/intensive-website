Why Eidetic?

Pathways over specific information

By now it's no secret that the way we process and store information is changing. The digital era has warranted prioritizing remembering how to find information over the specific information itself.

Much like memory recall in the brain is triggered by external mementos, Eidetic will use contextual information to try to guess what item you might want to be seeing next. With enough customization, metadata, and some trial and error, we believe the app can revolutionize the way you think about the storage or your most important memories.
